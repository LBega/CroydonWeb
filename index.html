There are many boroughs of London however, few have as interesting a history or as culturally diverse as Croydon. Since 1276 It has grown exponentially into the town it is today and its current population is 385,346 according to the survey in 2018 which is more than the population of Iceland! There are many enjoyable activities for both young and old such as the many museums and heritage sites located around the town.
